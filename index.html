My name is Derek.

I'm an applied statistician who helps managers deliver better products and experiences through the scientific study of choice.

Small Data, Big Insights

Small data can be just as effective as Big Data but it requires a more tailored approach. With carefully designed choice experiments, I can help you learn why people choose (or don't choose) you over the competition. These insights allow you to allocate scarce company resources and put them to use where they will have the greatest impact on your bottom line.

Choice Simulators

Built on a foundation of Nobel-prize-winning theory and Bayesian statistics, choice simulators offer you a glimpse into parallel worlds where you are the scientist: changing variables and seeing how people respond. Choice simulators will help you stay innovative and learn how to compete in the market with confidence.
