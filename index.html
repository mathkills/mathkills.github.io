I'm Derek Miller, a statistician who runs choice experiments and builds simulation tools to help people make better decisions.

Simulation Science

The most important business problems can only be solved with a human in the loop. Instead of leaving decisions to statistical models, choice simulators give you the power to ask questions. Leveraging data from carefully designed conjoint experiments, you can simulate how people's choices change in response to changes in price, product, promotion, or place.
