Choose Wisely

Math is a powerful tool. When used responsibly, it can be extremely helpful. On the other hand, it's easy to jump to the conclusions and really screw things up. That's where I come in. I'll work with you to make sure the models you implement in your business are built on solid, rigorous principles with diagnostics and tests to make sure that you don't mess something up.

Predict Responsibly

Doing math on paper is hard enough. If you don't have anxiety from that then you certainly will when it comes to applying that math to the real world on real data. Implementing math has real implications and it's stressful when the cost of failure is high. Simulation and scenario building is a way to make modeling playful and fun and way less stressful. It also helps provide you with a little more certainty before you deploy something in the real world.
