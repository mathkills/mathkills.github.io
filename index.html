I'm Derek Miller, a statistician who runs choice experiments and builds simulation tools to help people make better decisions.

Pricing + Product Research

Pricing is super easy: just find where supply equals demand! Or so we are taught in Economics 101. Anyone actually doing business knows that pricing is deceptively complex. Studies show that setting the wrong price can have a dramatic impact on your bottom line. I can help you improve your products and prices with proven methodologies like conjoint analysis, maxdiff analysis, A/B testing, and so much more.

Choice Simulators

The most important business problems can only be solved with a human in the loop. Choice simulators give you the power to ask the questions you wish you knew the answers to. Built on a foundation of Nobel prize-winning theory, Bayesian statistics, and experimental design, choice simulators offer you a glimpse into parallel worlds where you are the scientist, changing variables and seeing how people respond.
