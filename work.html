Compute Responsibly

Conjoint analysis is a powerful tool for making better business decisions. However, it's easy to jump to conclusions and really screw things up. That's where I come in. I'll work with you to make sure the experiments and analyses you implement in your business are built on solid, rigorous principles with diagnostics and tests to make sure that you don't mess something up.

Wise Choice

Simulation and scenario building is a way to make modeling playful and fun and reduces stress by giving you answers to what-if questions. Studying people's choices with discrete choice experiments such as conjoint analysis and A/B testing can provide you with a little more certainty before you deploy something in the real world.
